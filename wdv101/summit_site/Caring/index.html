How to Care for your Dog

Training

  • Postive Reinforcement is the most effective way of training these types of dogs
  • Theses kinds of dogs are good around families but should be socialized from a young age
  • These dogs do well with a set schedule that doesnt change, I.E. eat at the same time everyday

Grooming

  • Make sure to brush your dog often, these dogs have a lot of hair. Aim for once a weak
  • look out for sheading, these dogs have undercoats so they will shead a lot when it gets warmer out
  • like said above these dogs have undercoats, so you will most likely have to remove fur
  • You can always take your dog to a groomer if you don't know what to do

Health

  • You need to watch your dogs diet
  • Make sure they gets lots of exercise
  • Groom them regularly
  • Take them to the vet if you think anything is wrong
  • Watch the heat, these dogs where bred for cold enviroments
