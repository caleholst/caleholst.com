About Summit Farms

  • We have been open for 19 years
  • We have adopted more than 2,620 Dogs
  • We believe in helping the helpless and reducing the number of homeless dogs while improving the welfare of all domestic animals
  • We believe that all life is valuable and worthy of protection.

Facts about the Dogs

  • Alaskan Husky isn't technically a dog breed
  • Alaskan Malamute's date to back 5000 years ago
  • Alaskan Klee Kai's foundation breed is A Alaskan Husky
  • American Eskimo Dog's originate from Germany
  • The Siberian Husky was bred by the Chukchi people to be a sled dog
  • the Samoyed is another breed form Siberia, they where bred to herd raindeer
  • Greenland dogs are also known as Greenland Husky or Gronlandshunden
  • Seppala Siberian Sleddog used to be the same breed as a Siberian Husky, but husky where later bred for looks while this dog contiued to be bred for sleds
